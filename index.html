Everything you need to know about using the Rewards & Recognition Portal — for users and admins alike

Submission Timelines & Deadlines
Deadlines for each award cycle — both Quarterly Awards and Star of the Month — are announced and shared with all team members well in advance before the cycle opens. You will receive a communication via email and/or team channels with the exact open date, submission window, and closing deadline for that period.

Please do not wait until the last day. Nominations submitted close to the deadline may not receive the attention they deserve. We encourage everyone to nominate as soon as they witness outstanding work worth celebrating.

📣 Watch out for the official announcement emails from the BOI R&R Admin Team for upcoming cycle dates.

1 — What is This Portal?

The Accenture BOI Rewards & Recognition (R&R) Portal is the official platform for celebrating exceptional work within the BOI project team. It gives every team member an easy way to formally nominate colleagues who have gone above and beyond — and gives admins the tools to review, approve, and rank those nominations.

🏆
Quarterly Awards
Recognise standout individual or team performance over a full quarter. Multiple categories available — Client Focus, Innovation, Collaboration, and more.
⭐
Star of the Month
A monthly spotlight for someone who truly stood out — quick, impactful, and easy to submit. Great for recognising day-to-day excellence.
📋
Transparent Tracking
All submissions are visible to registered users. You can view statuses (Pending / Approved), filter, search, and download reports at any time.
🔐
2 — Logging In & Your Account
1 Enter your Accenture email

On the login screen, type your full @accenture.com email address and click Continue. Only pre-registered Accenture emails are accepted — if yours hasn't been registered yet, contact an Admin.

2 Enter your password

Your initial password is set by the Admin when they register your account. You can change it at any time using the "Change Password" link on the login screen before signing in.

💡 First time logging in? Use the initial password provided by your Admin. You are strongly encouraged to change it immediately using the Change Password link. Passwords must be at least 8 characters. If you ever forget your password, contact an Admin who can reset it for you from the Admin panel.
✍️
3 — Submitting a Nomination
🏆 Quarterly Awards Nomination
1

Select a Category — Choose from the award categories displayed at the top of the Quarterly Awards tab (e.g. Client Focus, Innovation, Collaboration, Team Award, etc.). Click the category card to select it; it will highlight in purple.

2

Fill in the Nominee Details — Enter the nominee's full name (letters only) and their @accenture.com email. For Team Awards, use the primary nominee fields for the main member and click "＋ Add Member" to add additional team members (each needs a unique name and email).

3

Team Name & Sub Team — Provide the team name (e.g. AMS, Data Solution, Shared Services, CIF) and the sub-team if applicable (e.g. Data Engineering, EDL, Testing, PMO). Do not mention the Squad Name.

4

Write the Reason / Citation — This is the most important part. Describe specifically what the person did, the impact it had, and the behaviour they demonstrated. Be concrete and detailed. You have up to 1,500 characters. A strong citation significantly increases the chance of approval.

5

Submit — Click "Submit Nomination →". You will see a confirmation toast notification. Your submission will immediately appear in All Submissions with a Pending status.

⭐ Star of the Month Nomination

Star of the Month nominations follow the same process as above — fill in the nominee's name, email, team, sub-team, and reason — but there is no category selection required. Go to the ⭐ Star of the Month tab and complete the form directly.

✅ Tip: Star of the Month nominations are ideal for recognising someone who made a difference right now — a great client interaction, clutch delivery, or quietly keeping things running. You don't need to wait for the quarterly cycle.
⚠ Important: You can only nominate registered @accenture.com users. Nominations are submitted under your logged-in identity. You can edit your own nominations at any time from the All Submissions tab as long as they are still in Pending status.
📋
4 — Viewing All Submissions

The All Submissions tab is the central view of every nomination that has been submitted. All registered users can view this page. Here's what you can do:

🔍
Search
Type in the search box to filter nominations by nominee name instantly.
🎛
Filter Buttons
Use the quick filter buttons to view All, Quarterly only, Star/Month only, Pending only, or Approved only.
▼
Column Filters
Click the ▼ icon on any column header (Type, Category, Team, Status, etc.) to filter by specific values — works like an Excel filter.
⬇
Download Report
Click "⬇ Download Excel Report" to export all currently visible (filtered) nominations as an Excel file.
👁
View Details
Click the View button on any row to open the full nomination details, including the complete citation text.
✏️
Edit Your Nominations
You can edit nominations you submitted as long as they are still in Pending status. Click Edit on your row to make changes.
Status Guide — Pending means the nomination is awaiting admin review. Approved means it has been reviewed and finalised by an admin. An approved entry may also have a Rank assigned.

5 — Admin Guide
This Section is visible to Admin only & meant for Admin-role users only
🔒 Admin Only

The Admin panel is only accessible to users registered with the Admin role. Non-admin users will see a locked screen when visiting the Admin tab. Below is a full walkthrough of every admin function.

☁ Data Sync

The Data Sync panel is the first thing you'll see in the Admin tab. Use "🔄 Sync Nominations" to pull the latest nomination data from the portal backend, and "🔄 Refresh Users" to reload the user list. Always sync after onboarding new users or if you suspect the display is stale. The status line below the heading confirms the last sync result.

👥 User Management

Manage who can access the portal. Only registered @accenture.com users can log in — no self-registration is possible.

A

Register a New User — Click "＋ Register New User", enter their Accenture email, full name, initial password, and role (User or Admin). Share the initial password securely with the new user and advise them to change it on first login.

B

Edit a User — In the user table, click Edit next to any user to update their name, email, role, or reset their password. Use the search box above the table to quickly find a specific user.

C

Delete a User — Click Delete to permanently remove a user account. This prevents them from logging in. Their past nominations are unaffected.

D

Reset a User's Password — From the Edit User modal, use the Reset Password option to set a new password for the user. Communicate the new password to them directly and securely.

🏅 Approving & Rejecting Nominations

Admins action nominations from the All Submissions tab, not the Admin panel itself. Each row in the submissions table has action buttons:

Approve

Click Approve on a Pending nomination. You'll be prompted for the Approval Passcode (case-sensitive). You may also optionally assign a numeric rank (1, 2, 3…) to the nominee. Once confirmed, the status changes to Approved.

Reject

Click Reject on an Approved nomination to revert it back to Pending. Requires the Rejection Passcode. Use this if an approval was made in error or requires further review.

Delete

Any admin can permanently delete a nomination using the Deletion Passcode. This is irreversible — use it only when a submission needs to be removed entirely (duplicate, error, inappropriate content).

🔑 Passcode Note: All approval, rejection, and deletion actions are passcode-protected. Passcodes are case-sensitive and are maintained by the Admin team. If you are an admin and do not have the passcode, contact the Portal Product Owner.
🔐 Passcode Operations Summary
Approval Passcode
Used to approve a nomination and optionally assign a rank
Rejection Passcode
Used to revert an approved nomination back to Pending
Deletion Passcode
Used to permanently delete any nomination from the system
Flush Passcode
Danger Zone — wipes all submissions. Irreversible. Use only at end of cycle.
⚠ Danger Zone — Flush All Entries

The "🗑 Flush All Entries" button in the Admin panel permanently deletes every single submission across all categories and award types. This action cannot be undone. It requires the Flush Passcode and should only be used at the end of an award cycle once all results have been recorded and communicated externally.

6 — Frequently Asked Questions
Can I nominate myself? ＋

No. The portal is designed for peer and manager recognition. Self-nominations are not in the spirit of the R&R programme and will be reviewed accordingly by the admin team.

Can I nominate the same person more than once? ＋

Yes, within the same cycle — as long as the nominations are for different award categories or award types (e.g. one for Quarterly and one for Star of the Month). Multiple submissions for the exact same category and nominee in the same period will be reviewed by the admin.

When will I know the results? ＋

Results are communicated by the Admin team after the submission window closes and the review process is complete. You can track the status of any nomination (including your own) in the All Submissions tab in real time. Approved nominations will have their status updated to Approved and may have a rank assigned.

I can't log in — what do I do? ＋

If your login fails, it's most likely because your email hasn't been registered yet, or your password is incorrect. Contact the Admin team to have your account created or your password reset. Only @accenture.com addresses are accepted.

When does the submission window open and close? ＋

Submission timelines are published in advance by the Admin team via email and team communications. There is no automatic lock in the portal — it is the nominator's responsibility to submit within the communicated window. Late entries may not be considered for the current cycle.

What makes a strong nomination? ＋

The best nominations are specific and impactful. Mention the exact situation, what the person did, how it benefited the team or client, and what behaviours they demonstrated. Avoid vague phrases like "always does a great job" — instead describe a concrete example. A well-written citation is the strongest advocate your nominee has.

Can an Admin see my password? ＋

No — absolutely not. Passwords are never stored in plain text anywhere in the system. At the moment you set or change your password, it is immediately converted into an encrypted hash using SHA-256 — a one-way cryptographic method. This means the original password cannot be read, reversed, or retrieved by anyone, including Admins. The only person who knows your password is you. If you forget it, an Admin can reset it to a new one — but they cannot see or recover your old one.
