Passcode Operations

Use the passcode prompt when clicking Approve, Remove from Finals, or Flush All. Passcodes are case-sensitive and must be entered exactly. For approval, you may also assign a rank to the nominee after the passcode is validated.

⚠ Danger Zone — Flush All Entries

Permanently deletes every submission across all categories. This cannot be undone.

🏅 Approve & Finalise Nomination

Enter the approval passcode to finalise this nomination. You may also assign a ranking. Passcode is case-sensitive.

🚫 Remove from Finals

Enter the removal passcode to un-approve this nomination. This will revert the status to Pending.

⚠ Flush All Entries

This will permanently delete all submissions across all categories and types. This action cannot be undone. Enter the flush passcode to proceed.
